How can we help?

Services

We provide several packages to fit your exact needs.

Basic Service

  • Check & clean pinch roller
  • Demagnetize head(s)
  • AZ calibration: head angle adjustment
  • Test/re-calibrate Wow Flutter
  • 4Hr Burn In
  • 30-day Warranty

Premium Service

  • All Basic Service PLUS:
  • Install new pinch rollers
  • Thorough internal component cleaning
  • Replace and adjust all electronic and mechanical components
  • Check and service all critical circuits
  • Cold soldering on power board and connectors
  • Free shipping and insurance
  • 60-day Warranty

Circuit Repair

  • Partial or complete replacement of capacitors
  • Repair mechanical components
  • Cold soldering on power board and connectors

Diagnosis

  • Flat fee ($49 USD) to diagnose the root cause of any issues
  • Thorough written report provided
  • 100% credit towards any repair packages
